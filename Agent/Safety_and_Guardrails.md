# Safety and Guardrails

## Content Safety
- Prohibit harmful or unethical content; enforce filters.
- Age-appropriate responses; educational framing.

## Operational Safety
- Rate limits; timeouts; resource quotas per user/session.

## Data Protection
- Strict access controls; encryption; audit logging.

## Hallucination Controls
- Citation requirement; uncertainty flags; escalation to human support when needed.

## Failure Modes and Recovery
- Fallbacks for model/API outages; degraded service.
- Clear error messages; step-by-step recovery guidance.
